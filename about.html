About the Platform

Why this platform was created

AccessLearn was developed to support students affected by the digital divide, especially learners who depend on mobile devices, limited data, and slow or unstable internet.

Research-based design

The platform is based on the idea that educational technology should be accessible, simple, and inclusive. Many learning systems use heavy content that is difficult to access in low-resource environments.

AccessLearn responds to this challenge by prioritising low-bandwidth access, simple layout, readable design, downloadable content, and mobile compatibility.

Mission

To reduce educational inequality through accessible digital learning design.

Vision

To make web-based learning more inclusive for disadvantaged learners.

Focus

Low-bandwidth learning, accessibility, simplicity, and digital inclusion.
