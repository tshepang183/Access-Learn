# Stakeholder Analysis – AccessLearn

## Overview
This document identifies key stakeholders of the AccessLearn platform, including their roles, concerns, pain points, and success metrics.

---

## Stakeholder Table

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|------------|------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| Students | Primary users of the platform | Easy access to learning materials, low data usage | Expensive data, slow internet, difficult platforms | Faster access to lessons, reduced data usage |
| Lecturers | Provide learning content | Ability to deliver accessible content | Platforms not optimized for low-bandwidth users | Increased student engagement |
| System Developer | Builds and maintains system | System performance and usability | Managing features with limited resources | Stable system performance |
| University Administration | Oversees academic systems | Equal access to education | Digital inequality among students | Improved student participation |
| IT Support Staff | Maintain system operations | System reliability and uptime | Limited infrastructure in rural areas | Reduced downtime |
| Government / Policy Makers | Promote digital inclusion | Bridging digital divide | Lack of accessible education systems | Increased digital inclusion rates |

---

## Summary
The system must balance accessibility, performance, and usability to meet the needs of all stakeholders, especially students in low-resource environments.
