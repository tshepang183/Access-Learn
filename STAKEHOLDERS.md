# Stakeholder Analysis – Smart Logistics & Delivery System

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|------------|------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| Customers | Receive packages | Fast and reliable delivery | Late deliveries, lack of tracking | Deliveries completed on time (95%) |
| Drivers | Deliver packages | Efficient routes and schedules | Poor route planning, delays | Reduced delivery time by 20% |
| Warehouse Staff | Manage packages | Accurate package handling | Manual errors, lost packages | 99% package accuracy |
| System Admin | Manage system | System stability and uptime | System crashes, security issues | 99.9% uptime |
| Company Managers | Oversee operations | Cost efficiency and performance | High operational costs | Reduced delivery costs by 15% |
| IT Support | Maintain system | Easy maintenance and updates | Difficult debugging and updates | Faster issue resolution time |
| Dispatchers | Assign deliveries | Efficient driver allocation | Poor coordination | Faster assignment time |
